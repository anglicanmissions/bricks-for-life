What your "Bricks" buy...

$50 - The Seed Brick

Provides high-yield, drought-resistant seeds and fertilizer for one family for an entire season.

$150 - The Water Brick

Funds 20 meters of concrete piping to redirect water from evaporation-prone ditches to crop rows.

$500 - The Enterprise Brick

Equips the soap-making startup with raw materials and tools to employ local women.

Amlaku's Soap Enterprise

Beyond agriculture, Amlaku is empowering the women of Baregota. By launching a local soap production line, the community is tackling hygiene-related diseases while creating a sustainable income stream that stays within the village.
