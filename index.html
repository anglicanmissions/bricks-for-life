The Student.

Amlaku Yaregal escaped a life on the streets at age 12 to earn a degree in Plant Science. His return to Baregota marks the beginning of a community-wide transformation.

02

The Science.

By introducing concrete irrigation and precision row-planting, Amlaku converted seasonal scarcity into a consistent, bankable surplus for the village.

03

The Ripple.

The "Bricks" of knowledge are shared across village borders. Amlaku now mentors farmers in surrounding districts, scaling the impact throughout the region.
