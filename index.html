Project
Insights.

Are donations tax deductible?
Yes. Bricks for Life is managed by AWA, making it eligible for tax credits. Official receipts are issued automatically upon transaction.
How is progress monitored?
Agricultural yields and hydrological flow data are reported seasonally. Supporters receive Technical Reports via our newsletter.
What is the long-term goal?
By 2026, we aim for total community self-sufficiency, where Baregota serves as a training hub for neighboring districts.
