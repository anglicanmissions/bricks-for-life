Why Northern
Ethiopia?

A historically troubled country, Ethiopia is reckoning with a legacy of drought and famine. In the village of Baregota, outdated farming meant yielding poor harvests year after year.

Baregota’s population of 500+ lived in a cycle of "the hunger gap." Bricks for Life offers a dignified, consistent way to assist them through science, not just charity.

Project Support & FAQ
Are donations tax deductible?
Yes! Because Bricks for Life is run by AWA (our aid and development branch), it is eligible for tax credits. You will receive a receipt automatically.
How much goes to the project?
90 percent of your donation goes directly towards the project. We retain 10 percent for AWA operational costs only. No funds are used for religious purposes.
How are outcomes measured?
We update outcomes via our newsletter and the "Road to 2025" plan. Amlaku provides regular data on seasonal yield increases and canal efficiency.
