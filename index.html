Amlaku Yaregal lost his mother at birth and was raised by his sister.

At 12, he ran away to pursue an education, living on the streets and doing odd jobs to survive.

Eventually, he ended up as a tour guide to the carved rock churches of Lalibela, where he met Ric Foxley.

Today, with a degree in Plant Science, he has returned home to Baregota to teach vital agriculture, irrigation, and farming skills.
