The Visionary: Amlaku Yaregal

At age 12, Amlaku ran away from home to pursue an education, living on the streets before eventually securing a degree in Plant Science.

Today, he is a beacon of hope in Baregota. He trains farmers in row-planting, manages irrigation projects, and is now pioneering the Baregota Soap Enterprise to support local women and hygiene.
