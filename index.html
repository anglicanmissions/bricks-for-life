The Context

Hope in the Shadow
of Conflict

Northern Ethiopia has faced the devastating effects of civil unrest. For a village like Baregota, the war hasn't just brought instability—it has disrupted supply chains and food security.

"In times of conflict, external aid can be fragile. Local resilience is the only permanent solution."

The Narrative

Three Chapters of Change

01

The Runaway Student

Amlaku Yaregal fled homelessness at age 12 to earn his degree in Plant Science. His education became the foundation for everything that followed.

02

Closing the Hunger Gap

Returning to Baregota, Amlaku implemented row-planting and concrete irrigation. He turned seasonal food shortages into a year-round surplus.

03

The Ripple Effect

Impact doesn't stop at the border of Baregota. Amlaku is now training farmers in neighboring villages, ensuring that these "bricks" of knowledge build a resilient region.
