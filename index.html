Amlaku Yaregal lost his mother at birth. At 12, he ran away to seek education, surviving on the streets of Lalibela.

His meeting with Ric Foxley transformed a faith-filled question into a regional blueprint for sustainability.

Ric and friends funded Amlaku’s Plant Science degree. Today, he teaches scientific agronomy to his community.

Improved crops now testify to the project's value. Baregota is now a training hub for neighboring villages.
