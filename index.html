FAQ.

Are donations tax deductible?
Yes. Bricks for Life is managed by AWA (Aid and Development), making it eligible for tax credits. Official receipts are issued automatically upon transaction processing.
How much actually reaches the project?
90% of all contributions are deployed directly to project infrastructure, materials, and local labor. 10% is retained strictly for AWA operational management.
How is progress monitored?
Agricultural yields and hydrological flow data are reported seasonally. Supporters receive biannual Technical Reports via our newsletter.
