Amlaku Yaregal lost his mother at birth and was raised by his sister. At 12, he ran away to seek education, surviving on the streets of Lalibela.

Living in the streets and doing odd jobs, he eventually became a guide. In the rock churches, he met Ric Foxley. His faith-filled question became the seed for Bricks for Life.

Ric and others funded Amlaku’s tertiary studies in Plant Science, so that he could return home to Baregota and teach vital agriculture, irrigation and farming skills.

Today, the community has committed to his techniques. He is now training farmers in neighbouring villages, scaling the impact across the region.
