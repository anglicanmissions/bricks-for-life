Amlaku Yaregal lost his mother at birth and was raised by his sister. At 12, he ran away to seek education, surviving on the streets of Lalibela.

Living in the streets, he eventually became a guide. In the rock churches, he met Ric Foxley. His faith-filled question became the seed for Bricks for Life.

Ric and others funded Amlaku’s tertiary studies in Plant Science, so that Amlaku could return home to Baregota and teach vital agricultural skills.

Today, the community has committed to his techniques. He is now training farmers in neighbouring villages, scaling impact across the Highlands.
