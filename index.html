Amlaku lost his mother at birth and was raised by his sister. At 12, he ran away to seek education.

He became a guide in Lalibela, where he met Ric Foxley. His faith-filled question became the seed for Bricks for Life.

Funded for a Plant Science degree, Amlaku returned home to teach vital agriculture and irrigation skills.

Today, he trains farmers in neighbouring villages, scaling the impact across the Northern Highlands.
