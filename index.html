Meet Amlaku Yaregal

The Street Boy
Who Returned.

Raised by his sister after losing his mother at birth, Amlaku ran away at 12 to pursue education, eventually living on the streets of Lalibela.

With help from his friend Ric, Amlaku gained a degree in Plant Science, and is the engine behind Bricks for Life—returning to his village with vital agriculture knowledge.
