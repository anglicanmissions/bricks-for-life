From the streets to the fields.

The project was sparked by Amlaku Yaregal, who escaped homelessness to earn a degree in Plant Science. Instead of leaving his village behind, he returned to transform it.

  • Providing drought-resistant seeds and modern row-planting techniques.

  • Building water infrastructure to save 20-30% of water lost to evaporation.
