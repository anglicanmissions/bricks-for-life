Origin Story
“Please sir,
I want to go
to university.”

In the rock churches of Lalibela, Amlaku Yaregal asked Ric Foxley a question that would transform an entire village.

Ric and his friends funded Amlaku’s tertiary studies in Plant Science, so that he could return to Baregota to improve the lives of its inhabitants.
