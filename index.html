The Vision

Inspired by one faith-filled question.

Bricks for Life was created by Ric Foxley alongside Amlaku Yaregal. After funding Amlaku’s tertiary studies in Plant Science, he returned to his village in Baregota to improve the lives of nearly 500 inhabitants.

"At 12, Amlaku ran away to pursue an education, living on the streets. Today, he trains farmers in neighboring villages, creating a ripple effect of hope."

Foundational Services

Your donations build the "bricks" of a self-sustaining community.

Sustainable Agriculture

Teaching row-planting and providing high-quality seeds to end seasonal food shortages.

Water & Irrigation

Implementing concrete piping and water tanks to minimize loss and ensure year-round access.

Health & Hygiene

Supporting feminine hygiene access and a local soap-making business for economic independence.

Common Questions

Where does my money go? ▼

90% of your donation goes directly towards the project (seeds, fertiliser, piping). 10% is retained for operational costs by AWA. No funds are used for religious purposes.

Are donations tax deductible? ▼

Yes! Because Bricks for Life is run by AWA (Anglican Missions' aid branch), it is eligible for tax credits in New Zealand.
