One faith-filled question from Amlaku Yaregal to Ric Foxley changed the trajectory of the Highlands forever.

The visionary.
Amlaku Yaregal.

Raised by his sister after losing his mother at birth, Amlaku ran away at 12 to pursue education. Today, he returns as a plant scientist to lead his village toward self-sufficiency.

With help from Ric, he turned vital agricultural knowledge into the "bricks" of a community recovery.

01

Agronomy.

By switching from broadcasting to row-planting, we have tripled seasonal harvests and ended the local "hunger gap."

02

Irrigation.

3.5km of concrete canals shoring up the Highland water supply, protecting the land against heat and evaporation.

03

Independence.

The goal isn't charity; it's agency. Baregota is now a supplier, providing surplus crops to neighboring districts.
