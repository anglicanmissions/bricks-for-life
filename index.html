The Student.

Amlaku Yaregal escaped the streets at age 12 to earn a degree in Plant Science. His return to Baregota marks the beginning of a scientific revolution in local farming.

The Science.

By introducing concrete irrigation and precision row-planting, Amlaku converted seasonal scarcity into a consistent, bankable surplus.

Today, the "Bricks" of knowledge are shared across village borders. Amlaku now mentors farmers in surrounding districts, scaling the impact.
