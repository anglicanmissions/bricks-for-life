The Baregota Story

The Student who
Came Home.

At age 12, Amlaku Yaregal escaped a life on the streets to pursue a degree in Plant Science. His success wasn't a ticket out, but a tool to go back.

Closing the Hunger Gap

Amlaku identified that Baregota’s poverty wasn't a lack of effort, but a lack of infrastructure. By introducing row-planting and efficient irrigation, he turned subsistence into surplus.
